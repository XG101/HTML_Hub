Musica

Who doesn't love music. Music is one of the many forms of art that people often find themselves hours upon hours perfecting and creating

Regardless of who you are, someone who loves the feeling of action to someone who wishes to feel peaceful we all have our own meanings

and tastes in music

Music can be used for many reasons such as:

-To express the cultural standards of rythm, melody, and harmony

-To aid in a dancing experience

-To have shows or movies express emotion and feeling

-Something for people to listen for a feeling or emotion whenever they please
